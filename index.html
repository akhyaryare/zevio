FAQ

Everything you need to know.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use Zevio? ▾
None whatsoever. We handle the entire setup from start to finish — connecting your WhatsApp, configuring your AI, loading your menu and branding. You just show up and start using it. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Zevio.
How long does setup take? ▾
Most businesses are live within 24 hours of signing up. We book a setup call, configure everything together, and by the next morning your AI is live and answering customers.
Can I cancel at any time? ▾
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel with 30 days' notice at any time. We also offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers — if it's not for you, you'll get a full refund.
Will the AI replies sound like me? ▾
Yes. During setup we learn your tone, your common responses, your business personality. The AI is trained specifically for your business — not a generic bot. Your customers won't know the difference.
What WhatsApp number do customers message? ▾
Your existing business WhatsApp number. We connect to the WhatsApp Business API using your current number — your customers won't notice any change. All messages still appear in your WhatsApp app too.
Is my customer data safe? ▾
Completely. All data is stored securely in the UK and EU, encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share your data with third parties. We're fully GDPR-compliant.
What happens if the AI gets something wrong? ▾
You always have full visibility in your dashboard. The AI handles routine questions automatically, but you can review, edit or override any message. For complex situations, the AI flags them for you to handle personally.
Do you work with restaurants outside London? ▾
Absolutely. We work with businesses across the UK — London, Birmingham, Manchester, Leeds, Glasgow and beyond. Setup and support is all done remotely so location doesn't matter.
